Development

Our Software Engineers rapidly implement, debug and optimize ideas while maintaining good coding standards and writing elegant, high performing extensible code. We work well with existing codebases and we produce high-quality results due to high attention to detail and efficient prioritization.

At Ho Sinsaang, we work with a modern Technology Stack. For Web Development, our engineers prefer the MERN Stack (MongoDB/Mongoose, Express.js, React.js, Node.js). However, we are also experienced with PHP and related software such as Wordpress/WooCommerce - we will be happy to help you with your Legacy Project. For performance critical applications, we provide Best-in-Class C++ development with a strong focus on resource efficiency and scalabilty.
